Smart Lock

Enhanced security and keyless convenience. The Smart Lock allows you to secure and unlock your home remotely using your smartphone or a unique code. Grant access to guests or deliveries instantly, no matter where you are.

Features

  • Lock and unlock your door remotely via the SmartLiving app or using a keypad code.
  • Create temporary or recurring digital keys/codes for family and visitors.
  • View a detailed history of who entered and when, directly in the app.
  • Automatically locks the door after a set period for enhanced security.
