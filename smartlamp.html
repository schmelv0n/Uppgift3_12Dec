Smart Light Bulb

The Smart Light Bulb from SmartLiving allows you to control your home lighting from anywhere using your smartphone. With customizable colors, brightness levels, and scheduling features, you can create the perfect ambiance for any occasion.

Features

  • Control your lights from anywhere using the SmartLiving app.
  • Customizable colors and brightness levels.
  • Schedule your lights to turn on/off automatically.
  • Energy-efficient LED technology.
  • Compatible with voice assistants like Alexa and Google Assistant.
